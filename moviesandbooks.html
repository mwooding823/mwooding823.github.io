My Favorite Author: Mary Kubica

Some of my favorite books are written by Mary Kubica. Some examples of her books are "The Good Girl", "Local Woman Missing", and "Pretty Baby". She writes mostly mystery and suspense novels.

My Favorite Book: Local Woman Missing by Mary Kubica

This book was recently published in May of 2021. I read it over the summer after I had already read a few of Kubica's books. It is a suspense novel with many twists, and I highly reccommend it especially for those who have trouble sticking with a book.

My Favorite Movie: The Switch

This movie is a romantic comedy starring Jennifer Aniston and Jason Bateman. It is not widely popular or critically acclaimed, but it is a movie I could watch over and over again.
